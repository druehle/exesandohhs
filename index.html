Rules

  • Fill the 6x6 board with X and O.
  • Each row and each column must contain exactly three Xs and three Os.
  • No three matching pieces may appear in a row horizontally or vertically, including across board edges.
  • No two completed rows or completed columns may be identical.
  • A green = connector means the two neighboring cells must match.
  • A red ≠ connector means the two neighboring cells must be different.
  • Starting cells are fixed and cannot be changed.
  • Breaking a rule costs one life and highlights the violation; losing all lives ends the game.
